# Avel LLC — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Two Lanes, One Brand

---

## Lane 1: SaaS Companies Adding AI Features

**Who they are:**
- Founder or CTO of a B2B SaaS product
- 1–50 employees, post-MVP, have paying customers
- Feeling pressure to ship AI features but lack internal bandwidth or expertise

**Budget & Decision Making:**
- Budget exists — already spending on dev tools, contractors, infrastructure
- Decision maker is technical enough to understand the work but too busy to do it

**Pain Points:**
- Competitors are shipping AI
- Roadmap is backed up
- Can't hire fast enough

**Demographics:**
- US-based, remote-first
- Likely in a major tech hub or distributed team

**Where to Find Them:**
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- Indie hacker communities
- Product Hunt

---

## Lane 2: Multi-Entity Business Owners Needing AI Operations

**Who they are:**
- Owns 2–5 businesses, locations, or divisions
- $1M–$10M in revenue across their portfolio
- Non-technical but operationally sharp — they know their numbers

**Current State:**
- Running on spreadsheets, email threads, and disconnected software
- Has at least one high-cost manual process bleeding money every month

**Pain Points:**
- Can't see everything in one place
- Spends too much time managing instead of growing

**Demographics:**
- US-based, any city
- Industries: food service, healthcare, real estate, staffing

**Where to Find Them:**
- LinkedIn
- Local business networks
- Industry associations

---

## The Through-Line

Two very different buyers. Same outcome: their business runs better after you touch it.
